Welcome

I am currently a FONDECYT Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Mathematics at the Universidad de Concepción, Concepción, Chile, working with Prof. Ravi Prakash. Previously, I held a postdoctoral position at Fraunhofer ITWM, Germany, where I worked with Prof. Julia Orlik. Before that, I was an Institute Postdoctoral Fellow at the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research – Centre for Applicable Mathematics (TIFR-CAM), Bengaluru, India, under the mentorship of Dr. Debayan Maity. I obtained my Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru, where I was supervised by Prof. A. K. Nandakumaran.
